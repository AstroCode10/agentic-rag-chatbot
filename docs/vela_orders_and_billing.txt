VELA — ORDERS, BILLING AND PAYMENTS
=====================================

Subscription billing
--------------------
Vela subscriptions are billed in advance at the start of each billing period.
If you sign up on the 14th of the month, your billing date is the 14th of
every subsequent month (or year, for annual plans). Your billing date does not
change when you upgrade or downgrade.

Payment is processed automatically via the card saved on your account. If a
payment fails, Vela retries on days 3, 5, and 7. If all retries fail, your
account is moved to a restricted state: you can view data but syncing is paused
until payment is resolved. We will email you at each retry and when the account
is restricted.

Changing your plan
------------------
UPGRADING
You can upgrade from any plan to a higher plan at any time from Settings →
Billing → Change plan. Upgrades take effect immediately. You are charged a
prorated amount for the remaining days in your current billing period at the
new plan's price, minus the unused value of your current plan.

Example: you are on Growth (£79/month) with 15 days remaining in your billing
period. You upgrade to Pro (£199/month). You are charged:
  (£199 - £79) × (15 / 30) = £60 prorated charge

Your next full billing date remains unchanged.

DOWNGRADING
Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing period. You keep
access to your current plan's features until then. No refund or credit is
issued for the remaining days on the higher plan.

If downgrading would put you over the lower plan's limits (e.g. you have
8 channels connected and are downgrading from Growth to Starter which allows
3), Vela will warn you and ask you to disconnect channels before the downgrade
takes effect. If limits are still exceeded at the start of the new period,
syncing for the excess channels is paused until you resolve it.

Cancellation
------------
You can cancel your subscription at any time from Settings → Billing → Cancel
subscription. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing
period — you retain full access until then. You will not be charged again after
cancelling.

After your subscription ends:
- Your data (inventory, orders, suppliers) is retained for 30 days
- During those 30 days you can log in, export your data as CSV, and reactivate
- After 30 days your workspace and all data are permanently deleted
- This deletion is irreversible; Vela cannot recover deleted workspaces

If you cancel an annual plan, the remaining months are not refunded (except
within the 7-day refund window described below).

Refunds
-------
STANDARD REFUND POLICY
Vela offers a full refund if you request it within 7 days of your first paid
charge. This applies to the first payment after a free trial ends or after
a new account is created. To request a refund, email billing@getvela.com with
the subject line "Refund request" and include your account email address.
Refunds are processed within 5–10 business days to the original payment method.

After the 7-day window, we do not issue refunds for the current billing period.
This applies to monthly and annual plans alike. If you cancel an annual plan
after 7 days, no refund is issued for unused months.

EXCEPTIONS
We will issue a refund outside the standard policy if:
- Vela experienced a verified outage of 4+ hours during your billing period
  that materially impacted your use of the product (credit or refund at our
  discretion, proportional to the downtime)
- A billing error caused you to be charged an incorrect amount
- You were charged after cancelling (we will refund in full)

To raise an exception, email billing@getvela.com with your account details
and a description of the issue.

Updating payment details
------------------------
Go to Settings → Billing → Payment method to update your card at any time.
Changes take effect immediately and will be used for the next billing attempt.
If your payment has failed and your account is restricted, updating your card
and clicking "Retry payment" will immediately attempt to process the outstanding
charge.

We do not store full card numbers. Vela uses Stripe for payment processing;
only a tokenised reference to your card is stored on Vela's systems.

Invoices and receipts
---------------------
A PDF invoice is generated after every successful charge and is:
- Emailed to the billing email address on your account
- Available in Settings → Billing → Invoice history

Invoices include:
- Invoice number and date
- Description of the plan and billing period
- Amount charged and currency
- Your company name and billing address (if entered in Settings → Billing)
- Vela's company details and VAT number

To add a company name, VAT number, or billing address to your invoices, go to
Settings → Billing → Billing details. Changes apply to future invoices only;
past invoices cannot be reissued with different details.

VAT
---
Vela is registered for VAT in the United Kingdom (VAT number: GB 423 1987 62).
VAT at the current UK rate (20%) is added to subscriptions for customers whose
billing address is in the UK. EU customers with a valid VAT number can enter it
in Settings → Billing → Billing details to have VAT removed from their invoices
(reverse charge applies). Customers outside the UK and EU are not charged VAT.

Annual plans and invoicing
--------------------------
Annual Pro plans with a total value of £2,000 or more can be paid by bank
transfer on receipt of invoice rather than by card. Contact billing@getvela.com
to arrange this before signing up or upgrading. Payment is due within 14 days
of the invoice date. The subscription activates once payment is received.

Team billing
------------
Each Vela workspace has one billing owner. The billing owner is the person who
originally set up the subscription. Only the billing owner can change the plan,
update payment details, or cancel the subscription.

To transfer billing ownership to another team member, the current billing owner
must go to Settings → Team → (member name) → Make billing owner. Both parties
will receive a confirmation email.
