VELA — PRICING AND PLANS
========================

Overview
--------
Vela offers three paid plans and a 14-day free trial on all of them. There is
no permanently free tier. Pricing is per workspace (one business), billed
monthly or annually. Annual billing saves 20% compared to monthly.

Plans at a glance
-----------------

STARTER — £29/month (or £23/month billed annually)
Best for: solo founders or very small teams just getting started with inventory
management, selling on one or two channels.

Included:
- Up to 2 user seats
- Up to 3 connected sales channels
- Up to 500 active SKUs
- Up to 1,000 orders per month
- Inventory management (all core features)
- Order management (all core features)
- Supplier directory and purchase orders
- Low-stock alerts (email only)
- Basic reporting (inventory valuation, low-stock, sales velocity)
- CSV import and export
- Standard email support (response within 2 business days)

Not included on Starter:
- Profit margin reporting
- Custom email notifications via your own domain
- Slack integration
- QuickBooks / Xero accounting sync
- ShipStation / EasyPost shipping integration
- US data region
- Priority support

GROWTH — £79/month (or £63/month billed annually)

Everything in Starter, plus:

- Up to 10 connected sales channels
- Up to 5,000 active SKUs
- Up to 10,000 orders per month
- Profit margin reporting
- Custom email notifications via your own domain

Not included on Growth:

- Dedicated onboarding session
- Phone support

PRO — £199/month (or £159/month billed annually)
Best for: established businesses with high order volumes, larger teams, or those

Frequently asked questions — pricing
-------------------------------------

Q: Can I try Vela before paying?
A: Yes. All plans include a 14-day free trial with full access to the plan's
features. No credit card is required to start the trial. At the end of the
trial you'll be asked to enter payment details to continue. If you don't,
your account is paused (data retained for 30 days) and then deleted.

Q: What happens if I exceed my plan's SKU or order limits?
A: We do not cut off your account mid-month. If you exceed your limits, Vela
will notify you by email and prompt you to upgrade. You have until the end of
your current billing period to upgrade before syncing is paused. We never
delete data due to overage.

Q: Can I change plans at any time?
A: Yes. You can upgrade immediately — the price difference is prorated to your
remaining billing period. Downgrades take effect at the start of the next
billing period. You cannot downgrade mid-period.

Q: Is there a discount for annual billing?
A: Yes — 20% off the monthly price when you pay annually upfront.
Starter: £276/year (vs £348 monthly). Growth: £756/year (vs £948 monthly).
Pro: £1,908/year (vs £2,388 monthly).

Q: Do you offer discounts for startups or non-profits?
A: We offer a 30% discount for registered non-profit organisations. Email
billing@getvela.com with proof of non-profit status. We do not currently
have a formal startup programme, but contact us if you are pre-revenue and
we will do our best to help.

Q: Is there a per-user fee?
A: No. All plans are priced per workspace, not per seat. Growth and Pro
include unlimited users. Starter is capped at 2 users.

Q: What currency do you charge in?
A: Vela bills in GBP (£) by default. If your card is in a different currency,
your bank will apply its standard conversion rate. We do not add our own
currency conversion fee.

Q: Do you offer refunds?
A: We offer a full refund if you cancel within 7 days of your first paid
charge (not applicable to free trial periods). After 7 days, refunds are not
issued for the current billing period but you will not be charged again after
cancellation. See the Orders and Billing document for full details.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: Visa, Mastercard, and American Express credit and debit cards. We do not
currently accept PayPal, bank transfer, or invoicing (except for annual Pro
plans over £2,000 — contact billing@getvela.com).

Q: Can I get an invoice for my subscription?
A: Yes. PDF invoices are generated automatically and available in Settings →
Billing → Invoice history. They are also emailed to the billing email address
on your account after each charge.
