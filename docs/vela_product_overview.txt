VELA — PRODUCT OVERVIEW
=======================

What is Vela?
-------------
Vela is a cloud-based inventory and order management platform built for small
to medium e-commerce businesses. It gives store owners a single place to track
stock levels, manage orders across multiple sales channels, generate purchase
orders to suppliers, and view real-time business reports — without needing a
developer or an enterprise budget.

Vela connects to the sales channels you already use (Shopify, WooCommerce,
Etsy, Amazon) and pulls all your orders into one dashboard. When an item sells
on any channel, Vela deducts it from your central inventory automatically. When
stock runs low, Vela can alert you or raise a draft purchase order to your
supplier on your behalf.

Who is Vela for?
----------------
Vela is designed for:
- Solo founders and small teams (1–20 people) running online stores
- Businesses selling on more than one channel who are tired of managing
  spreadsheets or reconciling stock manually
- Store owners who have outgrown the basic inventory tools built into Shopify
  or WooCommerce but are not ready for enterprise systems like NetSuite

Vela is not designed for:
- Businesses with physical retail locations requiring full point-of-sale (POS)
  integration (we do not currently support POS)
- Businesses needing manufacturing or bill-of-materials (BOM) tracking
- Enterprise companies requiring on-premise hosting or custom SLAs

Core features
-------------
INVENTORY MANAGEMENT
- Central stock ledger: one source of truth synced across all connected channels
- Variant tracking: manage size, colour, and material variants as separate SKUs
- Bundles: define product bundles; Vela deducts component stock when a bundle sells
- Low-stock alerts: set reorder points per SKU; receive email or Slack notifications
- Stock adjustments: manually correct stock levels with an audit trail of who changed
  what and when
- Bulk import/export: upload or download your full inventory via CSV at any time

ORDER MANAGEMENT
- Unified order inbox: all orders from all channels appear in one list
- Order status tracking: pending, processing, shipped, delivered, returned
- Partial fulfilment: mark individual line items as shipped separately
- Returns and restocking: process returns and choose whether to restock the item
- Order notes: add internal notes to any order; notes are not visible to customers
- Bulk actions: update status, print packing slips, or export orders in bulk

SUPPLIER AND PURCHASE ORDERS
- Supplier directory: store contact details, lead times, and payment terms per supplier
- Purchase order generation: create POs manually or let Vela draft them automatically
  when stock falls below reorder points
- PO tracking: mark POs as sent, confirmed, partially received, or fully received
- Landed cost tracking: record shipping and customs costs against incoming stock

SALES CHANNELS
Vela currently supports native integrations with:
- Shopify (all plans)
- WooCommerce
- Etsy
- Amazon (FBA and FBM)
- eBay
- Wix eCommerce
Manual channel: enter orders by hand for markets, pop-ups, or phone orders

REPORTING
- Inventory valuation report: total stock value at cost and at retail price
- Sales velocity report: units sold per SKU per day, week, or month
- Low-stock report: all SKUs at or below their reorder point
- Supplier performance report: average lead times and order accuracy per supplier
- Profit margin report (Growth and Pro plans only): revenue minus cost of goods sold
  per SKU and per order

INTEGRATIONS
- Accounting: QuickBooks Online, Xero (sync invoices and cost of goods)
- Shipping: ShipStation, EasyPost (fetch rates, print labels, sync tracking numbers)
- Communication: Slack (low-stock and order alerts)
- Email: send order confirmations and shipping notifications via your own domain
  (Growth and Pro plans only)

TEAM AND ACCESS CONTROL
- Invite unlimited team members on Growth and Pro plans (Starter: up to 2 users)
- Role-based permissions: Admin, Manager, Fulfiller, Viewer
- Activity log: full audit trail of all changes made by any team member

Getting started
---------------
After signing up, the fastest path to value is:
1. Connect your first sales channel (takes 2–5 minutes; Vela guides you step by step)
2. Import your existing inventory via CSV or let Vela pull it from your connected channel
3. Set reorder points for your top 20 SKUs
4. Connect your shipping integration

Most customers have their first channel live and inventory synced within 30 minutes
of creating their account. A 14-day free trial is available on all plans with no
credit card required.

Mobile access
-------------
Vela has a mobile-optimised web interface accessible from any browser on iOS or
Android. A dedicated mobile app for iOS and Android is currently in development
and expected in Q3 2025. Push notifications for low-stock alerts and new orders
are available via the mobile web interface today.

Data and security
-----------------
- All data is stored in EU-West (Ireland) data centres by default
- US region available on Pro plan
- Data encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3)
- SOC 2 Type II certified (report available on request for Pro customers)
- Daily automated backups with 30-day retention
- GDPR compliant; Data Processing Agreement available on request

Uptime and support
------------------
Vela targets 99.9% uptime. Live status and incident history are published at
status.getvela.com. Planned maintenance windows are announced at least 48 hours
in advance and scheduled outside peak trading hours (weekday mornings, UTC).
